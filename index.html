Description

JB Construction Ltd was established in 1999 and is a tier one contractor offering construction services in terms of Site Analysis, Feasibility Studies, Preliminary Design Studies, Permit/Zoning Applications, etc. They handle various industrial, distribution, manufacturing, office, retail, recreational, healthcare and commercial projects in the south east area of England.

Our pledge is to establish lasting relationships with our customers by exceeding their expectations and gaining their trust through exceptional performance by every member of the construction team.
